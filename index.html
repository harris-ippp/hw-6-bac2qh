Mackinaw City

I drove to Mackinaw City, Michigan with a group of friends on October, 1st to see the red canopy. We rent a Chevy Suburban from Enterprise at Hydepark and set off around 11 am. About 7 hours later, we arrived at Mackinaw City, 407 miles away from Chicago.

It was my first time in Michigan after living in the Mid-west for five years and it was absolutely gurgeous.
